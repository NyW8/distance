Currency Converter

                     





Figuring out how to convert currencies can be difficult, especially in the moment. We use the Fixer API with the most recent exchange rate information to make the process simple, streamlined, and fast for on-the-go travellers. To get started, pick a starting currency from the dropdown menu and the amount of money you would like to convert. Use the second dropdown menu to select your intended conversion currency and click "Convert!". To convert in the reverse order, simply click the arrow in the direction you would like to convert.

Don't see a currency you need? Send us a suggestion and we'll make a change.
